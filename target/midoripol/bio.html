Welcome to the world of Midori Pol, where the electrifying fusion of alternative rock/metal and electronic music takes center stage. Pioneering a unique sound that pushes boundaries and defies genres, Midori Pol delivers an exhilarating concoction of raw energy and innovative beats. Created by the artist Paolo Montechiesi, Midori Pol seamlessly blends heavy metal riffs, thunderous drums, and effected vocals with pulsating electronic elements, resulting in a sonic adventure that captivates listeners. Driven by the desire to explore uncharted sonic territories, Midori Pol constantly tries to push the limits of musical experimentation inviting the listener to immerse themselves in an entirely new sonic experience. Immerse yourself in the pulsating rhythms and groundbreaking beats that Midori Pol has to offer.
